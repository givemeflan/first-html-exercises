How to Make a Sandwich

  1. First take out some bread from it's packaging, preferably sliced bread
  2. Take out 1 jar of mayo, some fried bacon, sliced tomatoes, lettuce, and a knife
  3. Lay a slice of bread on a plate and spread some mayo on it
  4. Next place lettuce, bacon, and sliced tomatoes on the slice of bread
  5. Top the sandwich with the slice of bread and it's complete! Bon Appetite!
